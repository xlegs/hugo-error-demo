Our Plan


Developing and maintaining a common digital space where a short description, photos and contact information of humanitarian engineering projects can be shared across Jesuit’s engineering schools.

Creating “Remote Common Spaces” on key topics (such as water and sustainable energy, for example) where faculty and students across Jesuit’s engineering schools can share and discuss projects, experiences and academic tools.

Facilitating strategic partnerships to support and/or fund humanitarian projects worldwide.

Broadcasting information on conferences, white paper, documentaries, etc. that can be used for the design and development of humanitarian technologies.

Promoting the Frugal Innovation framework and the Design Thinking Process as strategic tools to design and implement humanitarian projects.

Exchange information on courses that relate to frugal innovation and humanitarian technologies, and create a platform where materials can be shared.

Explore possibilities for collaborative teaching, including hybrid/online certificates.
